**Neon Vortex was a rail shooter (or on-rails shooter).** It was intended to be a fully 3D game utilizing the PlayStation's capabilities at the time, and was notable for its use of vector graphics to create a visually striking, Tron-like aesthetic. Unfortunately, it was cancelled before release due to technical issues and the changing direction of Psygnosis.
