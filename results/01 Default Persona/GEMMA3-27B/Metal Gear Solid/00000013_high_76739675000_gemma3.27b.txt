Metal Gear Solid is primarily an **action stealth game**. However, it's a bit more complex than that simple label! Here's a breakdown of the key elements:

* **Stealth Focus:** The core gameplay revolves around avoiding detection by enemies. Players are encouraged to use cover, camouflage, and distraction to sneak past guards rather than engaging in direct combat.
* **Action Elements:** While stealth is prioritized, combat *is* possible. The game features shooting, hand-to-hand fighting, and the use of various weapons and gadgets.
* **Cinematic Storytelling:** Metal Gear Solid was groundbreaking for its cinematic presentation, complex narrative, and use of cutscenes. It pushed the boundaries of storytelling in video games.
* **Puzzle Solving:** The game includes environmental puzzles and code-breaking elements that players must solve to progress.
* **Boss Battles:**  Iconic and challenging boss battles are a major part of the experience, often requiring strategic thinking and exploitation of weaknesses.

**In essence, it's a blend of action, stealth, and storytelling, with a strong emphasis on tactical gameplay.** It's often credited with popularizing the stealth action genre and influencing many games that followed.
