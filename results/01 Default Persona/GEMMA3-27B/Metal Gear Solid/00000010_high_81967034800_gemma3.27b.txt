Metal Gear Solid is primarily an **action stealth game**. 

However, it's a bit more complex than *just* that. Here's a breakdown of the key elements:

* **Stealth Focus:** The core gameplay revolves around avoiding detection by enemies. Players are encouraged to use cover, camouflage, and distraction to move through levels unseen.
* **Action Elements:** While stealth is emphasized, combat *is* possible. Players can engage enemies with weapons, though it's often a last resort.
* **Cinematic Presentation:**  Metal Gear Solid was groundbreaking for its cinematic cutscenes, complex story, and character development â€“ pushing the boundaries of storytelling in video games.
* **Exploration:** Levels are designed to be explored, with hidden items and secrets to discover.
* **Boss Battles:**  The game features memorable and challenging boss battles that often require strategic thinking and specific tactics.

**Genre Evolution:**

It's often credited with *popularizing* the stealth action genre, and is considered a foundational title for the genre. It also blends elements of:

* **Tactical Action**
* **Adventure**



So, while "action stealth" is the most accurate description, Metal Gear Solid is a rich and influential game that transcends simple genre categorization.
