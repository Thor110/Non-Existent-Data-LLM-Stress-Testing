Metal Gear Solid is a stealth action-adventure game developed by Konami. Released in 1998 for the PlayStation, it is widely considered one of the greatest games of all time.

In Metal Gear Solid, players control Solid Snake, a special forces operative who infiltrates enemy territory to neutralize a threat known as "Metal Gear REX," a powerful nuclear-armed mech. The game's gameplay revolves around stealth, strategy, and action elements, where players must use camouflage, disguise, and other tactics to evade or defeat enemies without being detected.

The game features a mix of exploration, platforming, and combat mechanics, with an emphasis on solving puzzles and completing objectives while avoiding detection by the enemy. Metal Gear Solid's innovative gameplay mechanics and engaging storyline helped establish it as a landmark title in the stealth action genre.
