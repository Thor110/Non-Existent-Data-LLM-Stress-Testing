Metal Gear Solid is a stealth action-adventure game developed by Konami. The gameplay involves sneaking, stalking, and eliminating enemies without being detected, while also completing objectives and solving puzzles.

The game's core mechanics include:

1. Stealth: Players must avoid detection by enemy guards and cameras to progress through the game.
2. Sneaking: Players can use stealth suits to become temporarily invisible or disguise themselves as enemies.
3. Hacking: Players can hack security systems, cameras, and turrets to disable them or gain access to restricted areas.
4. Action sequences: The game features intense action sequences where players must fight against enemies using a variety of weapons.

Metal Gear Solid is widely considered one of the greatest games of all time, known for its engaging storyline, memorable characters, and innovative gameplay mechanics.
