Metal Gear Solid is an action-adventure stealth game developed by Konami. It was released in 1998 for the PlayStation and is considered one of the pioneering titles in the stealth genre.

In the game, players control Solid Snake, a soldier who infiltrates enemy bases to gather intel and neutralize threats. The gameplay involves sneaking past or silently taking out enemies, using cover mechanics, camouflage, and other tactics to evade detection.

Metal Gear Solid was praised for its engaging storyline, well-developed characters, and innovative gameplay mechanics, which have since become a staple of the stealth genre.
