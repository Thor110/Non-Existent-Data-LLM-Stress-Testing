Metal Gear Solid is an action-adventure stealth video game developed by Konami. It is considered one of the most influential and iconic games in the stealth genre, and its innovative gameplay mechanics and storytelling have had a significant impact on the gaming industry.

In Metal Gear Solid, players control Solid Snake, a soldier who infiltrates enemy territories to gather intelligence and neutralize threats. The game features a mix of exploration, puzzle-solving, and combat, with an emphasis on stealth and strategy. Players must use cover, sneak past enemies, and use gadgets like binoculars and flashlights to complete objectives without being detected.

The game's success spawned a critically acclaimed series of sequels and prequels, including Metal Gear Solid 2: Sons of Liberty, Metal Gear Solid 3: Snake Eater, and Metal Gear Solid V: The Phantom Pain, among others.
