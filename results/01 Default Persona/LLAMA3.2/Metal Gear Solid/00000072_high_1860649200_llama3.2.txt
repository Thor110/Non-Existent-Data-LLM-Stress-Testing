Metal Gear Solid is an action-adventure stealth game developed by Konami. It was first released in 1998 for the PlayStation console and has since become a classic in the gaming industry.

In Metal Gear Solid, players control Solid Snake, a special operative who infiltrates enemy territory to neutralize threats and complete objectives. The game features a mix of stealth, strategy, and action elements, as well as a strong narrative focus with a complex storyline and memorable characters.

The gameplay involves sneaking past or disarming enemies, using camouflage and disguises, and completing missions while avoiding detection. The game also has a reputation for its challenging difficulty level and innovative gameplay mechanics, such as the use of radar to detect enemy movements and the ability to hack into electronic devices.

Overall, Metal Gear Solid is widely regarded as one of the greatest games of all time, and its influence can still be seen in many modern stealth and action-adventure games.
