Metal Gear Solid is an action-adventure stealth game developed by Konami. It combines elements of action games, stealth gameplay, and storytelling to create a unique gaming experience.

In the game, players control Solid Snake, a secret agent who must infiltrate enemy facilities and complete objectives without being detected. The game features a variety of stealth mechanics, such as camouflage, sneak attacks, and hacking, which allow Snake to evade detection and complete his missions.

Metal Gear Solid also incorporates elements of action games, including combat and platforming sections, as well as a strong narrative focus with cutscenes and character development. The game's story is known for its complex characters, intricate plot twists, and social commentary on themes such as war, politics, and identity.

Overall, Metal Gear Solid is considered one of the greatest video games of all time, and its blend of stealth, action, and storytelling has influenced many other games in the stealth genre.
