Metal Gear Solid is a stealth action-adventure game developed by Konami. The game was released in 1998 for the PlayStation console and has since become a classic in the gaming industry.

In Metal Gear Solid, players control the protagonist Solid Snake, who must infiltrate enemy territory to neutralize threats and prevent global catastrophe. The gameplay involves sneaking past or silently taking out enemies, using camouflage, stealth gadgets, and other tactics to evade detection.

The game's unique blend of stealth, strategy, and action elements helped establish it as a pioneer in the stealth genre, influencing many subsequent games and franchises.
