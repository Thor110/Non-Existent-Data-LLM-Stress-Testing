Metal Gear Solid is an action-adventure stealth video game developed by Konami. Released in 1998 for the PlayStation, it is widely considered one of the greatest games of all time.

In the game, players control Solid Snake, a soldier who infiltrates enemy territory to neutralize threats and uncover conspiracies. The gameplay involves exploration, stealth, and strategy, as Snake must use his skills and gadgets to evade detection by enemies while completing objectives.

The game's core mechanics include:

1. Stealth: Sneaking past or silently taking out enemies without alerting others.
2. Exploration: Navigating the environment, finding hidden paths, and uncovering secrets.
3. Action: Engaging in combat with enemies when stealth is impossible.
4. Puzzle-solving: Overcoming obstacles and challenges to progress through the game.

Metal Gear Solid's innovative gameplay mechanics, complex storyline, and memorable characters have made it a beloved classic among gamers.
