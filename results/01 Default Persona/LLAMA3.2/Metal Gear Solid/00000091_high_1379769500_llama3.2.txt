Metal Gear Solid is a stealth action-adventure game developed by Konami. It was released in 1998 for the PlayStation console and is considered one of the pioneering titles in the stealth genre.

In the game, players control Solid Snake, a soldier who infiltrates enemy territories to gather information and neutralize threats. The gameplay revolves around sneaking past or silently taking out enemies without being detected, using tactics such as hiding behind objects, using disguises, and exploiting ventilation shafts to move undetected.

The game's success spawned a successful franchise with numerous sequels, prequels, and spin-offs, cementing Metal Gear Solid's place as one of the most iconic and influential games in gaming history.
