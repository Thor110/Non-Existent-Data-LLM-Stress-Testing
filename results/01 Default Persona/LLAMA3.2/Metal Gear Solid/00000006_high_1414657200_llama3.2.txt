Metal Gear Solid is an action-adventure stealth game developed by Konami. It combines elements of stealth, strategy, and action to create a unique gaming experience.

In the game, players control Solid Snake, a soldier who infiltrates enemy territories to gather intelligence and complete objectives without being detected. The game features stealth mechanics such as hiding in shadows, sneaking past guards, and using disguises to evade detection.

The game also has elements of strategy, as players must use resources and equipment to overcome obstacles and defeat enemies. Additionally, the game has action-packed sequences where Snake engages in combat with enemy forces.

Metal Gear Solid is widely considered a classic game in the stealth genre and has influenced many other stealth games since its release in 1998.
