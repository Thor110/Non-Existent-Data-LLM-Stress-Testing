Metal Gear Solid is an action-adventure stealth game developed by Konami. Released in 1998 for the PlayStation, it is widely considered one of the greatest games of all time. The gameplay involves sneaking through levels undetected, completing objectives without alerting enemies, and engaging in combat when necessary.

The game's stealth mechanics, storyline, and characters have been highly influential in the gaming industry, and it has spawned numerous sequels, prequels, and spin-offs across various platforms.
