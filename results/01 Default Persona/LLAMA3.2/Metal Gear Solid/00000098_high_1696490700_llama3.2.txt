Metal Gear Solid is an action-adventure stealth game developed by Konami. Released in 1998, it is the fourth main installment in the Metal Gear series and is widely considered a classic in the gaming industry.

In Metal Gear Solid, players control Solid Snake, a secret agent who must infiltrate a heavily guarded facility to neutralize a terrorist threat. The game features stealth gameplay mechanics, such as camouflage, hiding spots, and sneaking past enemies undetected. Players can also use various gadgets and equipment, such as guns, binoculars, and hacking devices, to gain an advantage.

The game's narrative is known for its complexity and depth, exploring themes of war, identity, and the ethics of espionage. Metal Gear Solid received widespread critical acclaim upon its release and has since become one of the most influential games in the stealth genre.
