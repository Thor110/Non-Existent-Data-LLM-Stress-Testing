Metal Gear Solid is a stealth action-adventure game developed by Konami. It was released in 1998 for the PlayStation console and has since become one of the most iconic and influential games of all time.

The gameplay of Metal Gear Solid revolves around sneaking past or silently taking out enemies, using camouflage and disguise to remain undetected. The player must use stealth mechanics such as hiding behind objects, crouching, and using disguises to evade detection by enemy guards.

However, the game also features action-packed sequences where the player must fight against enemies in a more direct manner, using a variety of firearms and melee attacks.

Overall, Metal Gear Solid combines stealth gameplay with tactical combat elements, making it a unique and engaging experience for players.
