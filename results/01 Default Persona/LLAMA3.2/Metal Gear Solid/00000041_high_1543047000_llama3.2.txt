Metal Gear Solid is an action-adventure stealth video game developed by Konami. It was released in 1998 for the PlayStation console and has since become one of the most iconic and influential games of all time.

In the game, players control Solid Snake, a special forces operative who must infiltrate enemy bases and gather intelligence while avoiding detection by the game's enemies. The game features stealth mechanics, such as camouflage, lockpicking, and explosive devices, which allow players to complete objectives without being detected.

Metal Gear Solid was also notable for its storytelling, characters, and cinematic presentation, which set a new standard for video games at the time. It has since become a classic of the stealth genre and is widely regarded as one of the greatest games ever made.
