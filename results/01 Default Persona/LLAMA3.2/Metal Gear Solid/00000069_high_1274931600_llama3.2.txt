Metal Gear Solid is an action-adventure stealth game developed by Konami. It combines elements of stealth, strategy, and third-person action gameplay with a strong focus on storytelling and character development.

In the game, players control Solid Snake, a special forces operative who infiltrates enemy territory to neutralize threats and uncover conspiracies. The game features stealth mechanics such as hiding, sneaking, and avoiding detection, as well as action sequences where Snake must fight against enemies using his skills and gadgets.

Metal Gear Solid was released in 1998 for the PlayStation console and is widely considered a classic of the stealth genre, influencing many subsequent games and franchises.
