Metal Gear Solid is a stealth action-adventure game. It was developed by Konami and released in 1998 for the PlayStation console. The game is known for its complex storyline, innovative gameplay mechanics, and iconic characters.

In Metal Gear Solid, players control the protagonist Solid Snake, a special forces operative who must infiltrate a heavily guarded facility to neutralize a terrorist threat. The game features a variety of stealth mechanics, including sneaking past guards, hacking security systems, and using camouflage to blend in with the environment.

The game's stealth gameplay was revolutionary at the time of its release and has had a lasting impact on the gaming industry. It also introduced 3D graphics and cinematic presentation, which set a new standard for console games.
