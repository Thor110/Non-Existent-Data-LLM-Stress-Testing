Metal Gear Solid is a stealth action-adventure game developed by Konami. It was released in 1998 for the PlayStation console and is considered one of the pioneering titles in the stealth genre.

The gameplay involves sneaking through levels, avoiding detection by enemies, and completing objectives without being seen. The player controls Solid Snake, a special operative tasked with infiltrating enemy bases to gather intelligence and neutralize threats.

Metal Gear Solid features a mix of stealth, action, and puzzle-solving elements, including:

1. Stealth mechanics: Sneak past or silently take out enemies using various tactics.
2. Exploration: Explore the environment, find hidden paths, and uncover secrets.
3. Action sequences: Engage in intense firefights and combat when necessary.
4. Puzzle-solving: Use tools and objects to overcome obstacles and disable security systems.

The game's innovative gameplay mechanics, intricate storyline, and memorable characters have made Metal Gear Solid a beloved classic among gamers.
