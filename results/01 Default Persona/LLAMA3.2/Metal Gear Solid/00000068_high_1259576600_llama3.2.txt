Metal Gear Solid is an action-adventure stealth game developed by Konami. It combines elements of stealth, strategy, and action gameplay to create a unique experience.

In the game, players control Solid Snake, a soldier who infiltrates enemy territories to neutralize threats and complete objectives while avoiding detection by enemy forces. The game features a mix of stealth mechanics, such as sneaking, hiding, and disguising oneself, with more action-oriented elements like combat and vehicle driving.

Metal Gear Solid is often credited with popularizing the stealth genre in video games and has since become a classic among gamers and a influential title in the world of gaming.
