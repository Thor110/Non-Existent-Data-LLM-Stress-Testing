Metal Gear Solid is an action-adventure stealth game. It combines elements of stealth, strategy, and action to create a unique gaming experience.

In the game, players control Solid Snake, a special operative who must infiltrate enemy bases and neutralize threats without being detected by enemies. The game features a mix of exploration, puzzle-solving, and combat, with an emphasis on stealth and strategy.

The game's gameplay mechanics include:

1. Stealth: Players must sneak past or silently take out enemies to avoid detection.
2. Hacking: Snake can hack into electronic devices to disable alarms, access restricted areas, or gather information.
3. Camouflage: Snake can use camouflage suits to blend in with his surroundings and avoid detection.
4. Combat: When detected, Snake must engage in combat using a variety of firearms, grenades, and other tactics.

The game's success spawned a popular franchise with multiple sequels, prequels, and spin-offs, cementing its place as one of the most iconic and influential games of all time.
