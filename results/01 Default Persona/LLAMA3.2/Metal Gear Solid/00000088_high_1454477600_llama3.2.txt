Metal Gear Solid is a stealth action-adventure game developed by Konami. It was released in 1998 for the PlayStation console and is widely considered one of the greatest games of all time.

In the game, players control Solid Snake, a special forces operative who infiltrates a fortified enemy base to neutralize a threat to global security. The gameplay involves sneaking past or silently eliminating enemies, avoiding detection, and completing objectives while navigating through a large, open environment.

Metal Gear Solid is known for its innovative stealth mechanics, complex storyline, and memorable characters, which have become iconic in the world of gaming. It has since spawned numerous sequels, prequels, and spin-offs, cementing its place as a beloved franchise in the gaming industry.
