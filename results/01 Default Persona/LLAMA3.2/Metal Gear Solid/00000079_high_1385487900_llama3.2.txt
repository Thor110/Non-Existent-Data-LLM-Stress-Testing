Metal Gear Solid is a stealth action-adventure game developed by Konami. It combines elements of stealth, strategy, and action to create an immersive gaming experience.

In the game, players control Solid Snake, a secret agent who must infiltrate enemy territory, gather intel, and complete objectives while avoiding detection. The gameplay involves sneaking past or silently eliminating enemies, using camouflage, disguises, and other tactics to remain undetected.

The game features a mix of stealth-based puzzles, action sequences, and exploration elements, making it a unique blend of genres. Metal Gear Solid is widely considered a classic in the gaming industry and has spawned numerous sequels, prequels, and spin-offs.
