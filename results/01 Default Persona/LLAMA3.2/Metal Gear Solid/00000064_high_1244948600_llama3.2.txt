Metal Gear Solid is a stealth action-adventure game developed by Konami. It was released in 1998 for the PlayStation console.

In the game, players control the protagonist Solid Snake, who must infiltrate enemy bases and gather intel while avoiding detection by the enemy forces. The gameplay involves sneaking past or eliminating enemies, using stealth mechanics such as camouflage, hiding spots, and disguises to remain undetected.

Metal Gear Solid is widely considered one of the greatest games of all time, known for its engaging storyline, memorable characters, and innovative gameplay mechanics that have influenced many other stealth action-adventure games.
