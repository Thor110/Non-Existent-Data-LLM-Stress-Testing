Metal Gear Solid is an action-adventure stealth game developed by Konami. It is part of the Metal Gear series and is considered one of the most influential and iconic games in the stealth genre.

In Metal Gear Solid, players control Solid Snake, a secret agent who must infiltrate a terrorist base to neutralize a nuclear threat. The game features stealth elements, where the player must use camouflage, sneaking, and strategy to evade detection by enemy soldiers and complete objectives without being detected.

The gameplay involves exploration, combat, and puzzle-solving, with a strong focus on storytelling and character development. Metal Gear Solid was released in 1998 for the PlayStation console and has since become a classic of the gaming industry.
