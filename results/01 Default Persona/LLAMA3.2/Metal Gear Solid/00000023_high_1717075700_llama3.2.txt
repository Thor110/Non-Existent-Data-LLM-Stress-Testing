Metal Gear Solid is an action-adventure stealth game developed by Konami. The game combines elements of stealth, strategy, and action to create a unique gameplay experience.

In the game, players control Solid Snake, a soldier who must infiltrate enemy bases and complete objectives while avoiding detection by enemies. The game features a variety of stealth mechanics, such as sneaking past guards, using disguises, and hacking security systems.

The game also includes elements of action and combat, as Snake must fight against enemies when stealth is not possible or when he is ambushed. Additionally, the game features a narrative-driven storyline with cutscenes, puzzles, and other interactive elements.

Metal Gear Solid is often credited with popularizing the stealth genre in gaming and has had a significant impact on the industry. It is widely considered one of the greatest games of all time, and its influence can still be seen in many modern games today.
