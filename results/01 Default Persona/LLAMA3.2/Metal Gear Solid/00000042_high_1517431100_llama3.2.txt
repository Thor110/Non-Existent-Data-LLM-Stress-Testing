Metal Gear Solid is a stealth action-adventure game developed by Konami. The game's core mechanics involve sneaking, hiding, and disguising the protagonist, Solid Snake, as he navigates through enemy territories to complete objectives while avoiding detection.

The game features elements of:

1. Stealth: Players must use cover, concealment, and misdirection to evade or silently take out enemies.
2. Action-adventure: The game has a strong narrative focus with cutscenes, character interactions, and puzzle-solving elements.
3. Hacking: Snake can hack into electronic devices to access information, disable security systems, or create diversions.

Metal Gear Solid is widely regarded as one of the greatest games of all time, and its innovative gameplay mechanics have influenced many subsequent stealth-action games.
