Metal Gear Solid is a stealth action-adventure game developed by Konami. Released in 1998, it was the first game in the Metal Gear series and is widely considered one of the greatest games of all time.

The gameplay involves sneaking past enemies undetected, gathering information, and completing objectives while avoiding detection. The player controls Solid Snake, a stealthy operative, as he navigates through various environments, such as jungles, deserts, and urban areas, to complete his missions.

Metal Gear Solid features a mix of exploration, puzzle-solving, and combat, with an emphasis on strategy and stealth. The game's success spawned numerous sequels, prequels, and spin-offs, cementing its place in gaming history.
