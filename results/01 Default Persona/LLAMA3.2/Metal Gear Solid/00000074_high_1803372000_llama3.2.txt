Metal Gear Solid is an action-adventure stealth game developed by Konami. It was released in 1998 for the PlayStation console and is widely considered one of the greatest games of all time.

In the game, players control Solid Snake, a soldier who must infiltrate enemy territory to neutralize a terrorist threat known as FOXDIE (a fictional virus that can grant superhuman abilities). The game features a mix of stealth, action, and strategy elements, with a focus on sneaking past or silently eliminating enemies.

The gameplay involves navigating through levels, avoiding detection, and completing objectives while minimizing the use of loud noises and other attention-grabbing actions. The game also features a variety of gadgets and tools that allow Snake to interact with his environment and evade or defeat enemies.

Metal Gear Solid's innovative gameplay mechanics, engaging storyline, and memorable characters helped establish it as a classic in the action-adventure genre.
