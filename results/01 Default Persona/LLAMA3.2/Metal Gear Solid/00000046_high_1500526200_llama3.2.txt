Metal Gear Solid is a stealth action-adventure game developed by Konami. It was released in 1998 for the PlayStation console and is considered one of the greatest games of all time.

In the game, players control Solid Snake, a special forces operative who must infiltrate enemy territory to neutralize a terrorist threat. The gameplay involves sneaking past or silently taking out enemies, using stealth mechanics such as hiding, camouflage, and disguise, as well as utilizing advanced gadgets and equipment to evade detection.

The game's design emphasized strategy, patience, and exploration, requiring players to use their wits and reflexes to overcome challenges and complete objectives. This blend of stealth, action, and storytelling helped establish Metal Gear Solid as a groundbreaking title in the gaming industry.
