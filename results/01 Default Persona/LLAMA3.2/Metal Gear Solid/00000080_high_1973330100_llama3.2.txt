Metal Gear Solid is an action-adventure stealth video game developed by Konami. It is the third main installment in the Metal Gear series and was released in 1998 for the PlayStation console.

The game features a mix of exploration, puzzle-solving, and combat elements, with a strong emphasis on stealth gameplay mechanics. Players control Solid Snake, a special forces operative who must infiltrate enemy territory to gather intelligence and neutralize threats.

Some key features of Metal Gear Solid include:

1. Stealth gameplay: Sneaking past enemies undetected, using camouflage suits and disguises to avoid detection.
2. Exploration: Navigating through open environments, searching for hidden paths and secrets.
3. Combat: Fighting against enemy soldiers and bosses using a variety of weapons and tactics.
4. Hacking: Interacting with computer terminals and hacking into security systems to gain access to restricted areas.

Overall, Metal Gear Solid is an iconic game that has become a classic in the stealth genre, known for its complex gameplay mechanics, engaging storyline, and memorable characters.
