Metal Gear Solid is a stealth action-adventure game developed by Konami. It is part of the Metal Gear series and is known for its emphasis on strategy, stealth gameplay, and storytelling.

In Metal Gear Solid, players control Solid Snake, a secret agent who must infiltrate a terrorist-held island to neutralize a nuclear threat. The game features a variety of stealth mechanics, such as sneaking past guards, using camouflage, and disarming traps, all while completing objectives and unraveling the game's complex storyline.

The game is often credited with popularizing the stealth genre in video games and has since become a classic among gamers, influencing many other stealth action-adventure games.
