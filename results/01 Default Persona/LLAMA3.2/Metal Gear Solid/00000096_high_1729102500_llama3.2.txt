Metal Gear Solid is an action-adventure stealth game developed by Konami. The game combines elements of stealth, strategy, and action, with a strong emphasis on storytelling, character development, and player choice.

In the game, players control Solid Snake, a soldier who infiltrates enemy territories to gather intel and neutralize threats. The gameplay involves sneaking past or silently taking out enemies, using a variety of camouflage suits, gadgets, and equipment to remain undetected.

The game features a mix of stealth, action, and puzzle-solving elements, with an emphasis on exploration and strategy. Players must use their wits and reflexes to overcome obstacles, avoid detection, and complete objectives.

Metal Gear Solid is often credited as one of the pioneers of the stealth genre, and its influence can be seen in many subsequent games that have followed in its footsteps.
