Metal Gear Solid is a stealth action-adventure game developed by Konami. It was released in 1998 for the PlayStation console and is considered one of the most iconic and influential games of all time.

In the game, players control Solid Snake, a special forces operative who infiltrates enemy territory to gather intelligence and neutralize threats. The gameplay involves sneaking past or silently taking out enemies, using stealth mechanics such as camouflage, hiding spots, and non-lethal tactics to avoid detection.

The game features a mix of exploration, puzzle-solving, and action sequences, with a strong emphasis on storytelling and cinematic presentation. Metal Gear Solid's success spawned a beloved franchise with numerous sequels, prequels, and spin-offs, cementing its place in gaming history.
