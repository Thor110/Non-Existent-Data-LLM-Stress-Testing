Metal Gear Solid is a stealth action-adventure game developed by Konami. Released in 1998, it is considered one of the most influential and critically acclaimed games of all time.

In the game, players control Solid Snake, a special forces operative, as he infiltrates enemy territory to neutralize a threat known as the "Shadows." The gameplay involves stealth, strategy, and action elements, where Snake must use his skills, gadgets, and disguises to evade detection by enemies while completing objectives.

Metal Gear Solid introduced many innovative features that have become standard in the stealth genre, such as:

1. 3D gameplay
2. A focus on stealth and strategy over action
3. A variety of camouflage options and disguise abilities
4. A emphasis on storytelling and character development

The game's success spawned a series of sequels, prequels, and spin-offs, cementing Metal Gear Solid's place as a beloved franchise in the world of gaming.
