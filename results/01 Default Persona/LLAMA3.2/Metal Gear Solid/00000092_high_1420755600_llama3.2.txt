Metal Gear Solid is an action-adventure stealth video game developed by Konami. The game was released in 1998 for the PlayStation console and is considered a pioneering title in the stealth genre.

In Metal Gear Solid, players control Solid Snake, a secret agent who must infiltrate a heavily guarded facility to neutralize a threat to global security. The game features a mix of stealth, strategy, and action elements, with an emphasis on sneaking past enemies undetected, using disguises and camouflage, and completing objectives without being detected.

The game's innovative design and gameplay mechanics have had a significant impact on the gaming industry, influencing numerous other stealth games and setting the standard for future stealth games.
