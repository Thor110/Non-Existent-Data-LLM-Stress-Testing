Metal Gear Solid is an action-adventure stealth video game developed by Konami. It was released in 1998 for the PlayStation console and is considered one of the most iconic and influential games of all time.

The gameplay revolves around sneaking, stealth, and strategy, as players control Solid Snake, a protagonist who must infiltrate enemy bases to gather intel and complete objectives without being detected. The game features a mix of action, puzzle-solving, and exploration elements, with a strong focus on storytelling and cinematic presentation.

Metal Gear Solid is also notable for its use of 3D graphics, complex gameplay mechanics, and innovative controls, which helped to establish the stealth genre as a major force in gaming.
