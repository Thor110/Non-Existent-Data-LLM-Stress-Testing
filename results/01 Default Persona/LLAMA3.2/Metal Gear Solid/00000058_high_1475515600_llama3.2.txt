Metal Gear Solid is a stealth action-adventure game. It combines elements of stealth, strategy, and action to create an immersive experience for the player.

In the game, players control Solid Snake, a soldier who infiltrates enemy territory to gather intelligence and neutralize threats. The game's focus on stealth and strategy requires players to use camouflage, sneak past enemies undetected, and execute precise movements to avoid detection.

Metal Gear Solid was developed by Konami and released in 1998 for the PlayStation console. It is widely considered a classic of the stealth genre and has had a significant impact on the gaming industry as a whole.

The game's innovative mechanics, engaging storyline, and memorable characters have made it a beloved title among gamers and fans of the series.
