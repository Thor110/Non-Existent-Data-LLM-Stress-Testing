Metal Gear Solid is a stealth action-adventure game developed by Konami. It is part of the popular Metal Gear series and is considered one of the greatest games of all time.

In Metal Gear Solid, players take on the role of Solid Snake, a soldier who infiltrates enemy territory to gather intelligence and neutralize threats. The gameplay involves sneaking past or silently taking out enemies using stealth tactics, while also navigating through levels and solving puzzles.

The game's unique blend of action, stealth, and strategy elements helped redefine the gaming industry and set a new standard for 3D platformers and stealth games.
