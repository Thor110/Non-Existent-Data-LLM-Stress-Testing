Metal Gear Solid is a stealth action-adventure game. It was developed by Konami and released in 1998 for the PlayStation console. The game is known for its innovative gameplay mechanics, complex storyline, and emphasis on strategy and stealth.

In Metal Gear Solid, players control Solid Snake, a special forces operative who must infiltrate a heavily guarded facility to neutralize a terrorist threat. The game features a variety of stealth mechanics, including camouflage, sneaking past guards, hacking security systems, and using non-lethal takedowns to defeat enemies.

The game's success spawned a series of sequels, prequels, and spin-offs, as well as numerous other media, including novels, comic books, and movies. Metal Gear Solid is widely considered one of the greatest games of all time, and its influence can still be seen in many modern stealth action-adventure games.
