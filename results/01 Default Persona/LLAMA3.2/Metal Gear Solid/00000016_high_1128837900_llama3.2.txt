Metal Gear Solid is an action-adventure stealth video game developed by Konami. The game was released in 1998 for the PlayStation console.

In Metal Gear Solid, players control Naked Snake (also known as Big Boss), a soldier who infiltrates enemy territory to gather intelligence and complete objectives. The gameplay involves stealth, strategy, and action elements, where the player must use camouflage, sneak past enemies, and complete challenges without being detected.

The game was praised for its innovative gameplay mechanics, engaging storyline, and memorable characters, which have since become iconic in gaming culture.
