Metal Gear Solid is an action-adventure stealth game developed by Konami and released in 1998 for the PlayStation. The gameplay involves sneaking, hiding, and disguising as enemy soldiers to evade detection while completing objectives, such as infiltrating enemy bases or extracting valuable information.

The game features a unique blend of stealth, strategy, and action elements, with an emphasis on storytelling, characters, and cinematic cutscenes. Players control Solid Snake, a fictional operative working for the secret organization FOXHOUND, as he navigates through various environments to uncover conspiracies and stop the villainous plans of his enemies.

Metal Gear Solid is widely regarded as one of the greatest games of all time, influential in shaping the stealth genre and inspiring numerous sequels, spin-offs, and adaptations.
