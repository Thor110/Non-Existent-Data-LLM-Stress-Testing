Metal Gear Solid is an action-adventure stealth game developed by Konami. The game combines elements of stealth, strategy, and action, allowing players to control the protagonist, Solid Snake, as he navigates through various levels and completes objectives while avoiding detection.

In Metal Gear Solid, players can use a variety of tactics such as sneaking past enemies, hacking cameras and turrets, using stealth suits to become invisible, and engaging in combat when necessary. The game's storyline is heavily focused on the psychological aspects of war and the protagonist's character development.

Metal Gear Solid was released in 1998 for the PlayStation console and has since become a classic of the stealth genre, with numerous sequels, prequels, and spin-offs developed by Konami.
