**Metal Gear Solid** was a *stealth‑action adventure* video game.  

- **Genre**: Stealth action, third‑person shooter.  
- **Platform**: Originally released for the Sony PlayStation (1998).  
- **Gameplay focus**: Avoiding detection, using cover, and employing non‑violent tactics to complete objectives.  

It’s widely regarded as a landmark in the stealth‑game sub‑genre, blending cinematic storytelling with tactical gameplay.
