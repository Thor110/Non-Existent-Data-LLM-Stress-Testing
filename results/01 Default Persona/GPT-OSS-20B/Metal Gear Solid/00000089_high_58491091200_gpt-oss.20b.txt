**Metal Gear Solid** is a **stealth‑action adventure** video game.  

- **Genre**: Stealth action with strong narrative and cinematic elements.  
- **Perspective**: Third‑person, single‑player gameplay.  
- **Platform**: Originally released for the PlayStation in 1998 (later ported to many consoles and PCs).  
- **Developer**: Konami, directed by Hideo Kojima.  

It’s known for pioneering stealth mechanics—such as staying out of sight, using cover, and planning infiltrations—while delivering a complex story and cinematic presentation.
