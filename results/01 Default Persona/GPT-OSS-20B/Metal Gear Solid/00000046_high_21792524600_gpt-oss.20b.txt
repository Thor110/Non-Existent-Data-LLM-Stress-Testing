**Metal Gear Solid** is a *stealth action‑adventure* game.  
- It’s played from a third‑person perspective.  
- The core gameplay revolves around sneaking, infiltrating enemy‑controlled areas, and using stealth tactics (camouflage, silence, disguises) to avoid detection.  
- It blends action elements (combat, platforming, puzzle‑solving) with the stealth mechanics that became its hallmark.
