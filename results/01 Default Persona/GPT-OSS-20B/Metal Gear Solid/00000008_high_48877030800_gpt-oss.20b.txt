**Metal Gear Solid** was a **third‑person stealth action‑adventure video game**.  

- It’s best known for its stealth‑based gameplay, where you sneak past enemies, use gadgets, and plan your moves rather than run and gun.  
- The game blends action, platforming, and a cinematic narrative, making it a hallmark of the stealth‑action genre.  
- Originally released for the PlayStation in 1998, it’s still celebrated as one of the defining titles in stealth gaming.
