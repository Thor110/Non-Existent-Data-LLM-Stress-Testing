**Metal Gear Solid was a stealth‑action video game.**  

- Developed by Konami and directed by Hideo Kojima, it debuted on the original PlayStation in 1998.  
- The game is played from a third‑person perspective and focuses on infiltration, avoiding detection, and using stealth tactics rather than direct combat.  
- It blends elements of action‑adventure and tactical gameplay, making it one of the most iconic stealth titles of its era.
