**Metal Gear Solid** is a **stealth‑action adventure** video game.  
- It’s played from a third‑person perspective.  
- The core gameplay revolves around stealth—avoiding detection, infiltrating enemy bases, and using cover and timing rather than head‑on combat.  
- It blends action elements (shooting, hand‑to‑hand combat) with a strong narrative focus, making it a classic stealth‑action adventure title.
