Metal Gear Solid is a **stealth‑action adventure** video game.  

- **Genre**: Primarily a stealth game, but it also incorporates elements of third‑person shooting, puzzle‑solving, and cinematic storytelling.  
- **Perspective**: Played from a third‑person viewpoint, allowing you to see the character and the environment as you plan infiltrations.  
- **Platform**: First released for the PlayStation in 1998, it later appeared on many other consoles and PCs.  
- **Gameplay focus**: Avoid detection, use cover and gadgets, and complete objectives through careful planning rather than direct confrontation.  

So, at its core, Metal Gear Solid is a stealth‑action adventure that blends stealth mechanics with action‑driven gameplay.
